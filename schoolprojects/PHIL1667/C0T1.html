Description:

T-1000 is the main antagonist in Terminator 2: Judgement Day. It is a robot that was created by Skynet and sent back in time to kill John Connor before he could lead the rebellion in the future. It is a more advanced version of the T-800 and can reform itself into any shape of equivalent volume.

Connection to the Hero's Journey

The T-1000 starts in its ordinary world of the future until it is given the call to action by Skynet of killing John Connor. As such, it crosses the threshold into the past world. Here, as a T-800 is guarding John, it faces many challenges and trials. In the final battle, the T-1000 is melted in a steel foundry and fails in achieving its goal. Its journey ends here.
