Description:

Skynet is a computer system in Terminator 2: Judgement Day as well as in the rest of the franchise. It was made to defend Earth, but, after it gained sentience, decided that humans were in the way of this goal. So, it turned on its creator and is actively trying to destroy humankind. In an attempt to kill off the leader, it has sent back two terminators to try to kill or make sure John Connor, the leader of the resistance against Skynet, never exists.
