Connection to the Hero's Journey

In the beginning, Tanya is in her own world in the tail car of the train. The threshold is then crossed figuratively and literally with the escape from the tail car, starting the rebellion. Tanya then faces trials as she tries to find her son, and is killed in one of the trials. Before this happens, her association with Curtis allows her to achieve the goal of keeping Timmy safe.
