Description:

Weyland is a minor character in Ridley Scott’s Prometheus as well as an influence in other films of the franchise. He is the head of Weyland Corp, a massive company with the funds to send Holloway, Shaw and their team on an expedition to a star system far away. He has a secret motive of trying to ask the “Engineers” how to live forever.

Connection to the Hero's Journey

Weyland starts off in the high class, growing old with a lot of money. His call to action is a result of his old age, which makes him seek eternal life. As such he crosses the threshold by secretly joining the expedition to the planet of the “Engineers” in order to ask them how to live forever. His old age provides challenges to this, and he is ultimately killed by the people he wanted to ask, failing his goal and ending his journey.
