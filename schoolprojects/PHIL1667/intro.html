Thesis and Background

“Based on Campbell’s theory of the monomyth, many stories seen in sci-fi reflect real stories as well as each other, leading to similar character archetypes that permeate through worlds both real and made up.”

The point of this game/quiz is to show how real heroes and fictional ones can relate to the Hero’s Journey as described by Joseph Campbell. In writing out paths for many (42) of the characters seen in the films we have watched, and a brief overview of how their story relates to the Hero’s Journey. The purpose of the game/quiz format is to provide a platform for players/users to relate the character they have in their head to the one that they ultimately choose. It is to make them think whether or not they agree that this character really experiences a similar path as the one given after they answer questions.

Further Notice

It should be noted that the questions on this quiz and their answers reflect my beliefs when the film does not provide a clear black vs. white comparison. One example of this is how some characters sorted into the “high class” may be there because of rank rather than being really high class (they may be middle or high class). In addition, as there is so much content to write about, I may have gotten smaller facts about a character wrong, and the very nature of interpreting their journey is subjective.
