Why the year + 10000?

If you haven't noticed, all my dates have a 1 tacked on in front of the year. This is based off of an arguement that Kurzgesagt introduced in this video. I liked this idea, so, I am supporting it! Want to know the typical date? just delete the 1 in front of the year.
